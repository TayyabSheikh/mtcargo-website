Corporate Overview

MT-Cargo Division is one of Pakistan's most trusted and experienced logistics and customs clearance companies.

Founded in 1989, we manage complex supply chains, customs exemption procedures, and cross-border transportation. We serve international development agencies, government ministries, NGOs, and large-scale infrastructure projects, upholding a zero-tolerance policy for non-compliance.
